Dr. Nikos Goulas is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Shipping, Trade and Transport, University of the Aegean. His research focuses on maritime robotics, autonomous systems, and sensor fusion for navigation.

Thomas Kogias

Thomas Kogias is a PhD candidate and researcher at the University of the Aegean. His work involves the development of control algorithms for unmanned surface vessels and embedded systems integration for marine applications.

Nikos Sapountzis

nsapountzis@aegean.gr
